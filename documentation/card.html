Card Variations

Here is the card feature which allows you to pop information and make it more visible on any section of your website. Also, allowing you to add call to action as a part of it to let the user interact. 

                    
    
    <div class="swagoo-card">
        <div class="swagoo-card-header">
            This is my title
        </div>
        <div class="swagoo-card-body">
            Here is where we are going to 
            put in the body.
        </div>  
        <div class="swagoo-card-footer">
            <button class="swagoo-btn">Buy Now
            </button>
            <a href="#" class="swagoo-btn">Buy Now
            </a>
        </div>  
    </div>
